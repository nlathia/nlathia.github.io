Neal Lathia

Senior Research Associate, University of Cambridge

About

Dr. Neal Lathia is a Senior Research Associate in the Computer Laboratory at the University of Cambridge.

Previously, he was a an honorary Research Associate in the College of Behavioral and Social Sciences at the University of Maryland, a Research Associate in the Department of Computer Science at University College London, and a visiting researcher at Telefonica Research, and a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at University College London.

Neal's work focuses on data-driven applications. This work has spanned digital health monitoring, smartphone sensors, urban computing, online recommender systems, and the analysis of 'big' data sets of transport records, financial transactions, and online ratings. He obtained a PhD in Computer Science in 2010 from University College London: his doctoral work introduced methods for evaluating collaborative filtering algorithms over time.
